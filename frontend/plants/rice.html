Rice

Planting

  • Soil: Fertile clay loam with good water-holding capacity.
  • Needs standing water (paddy).
  • Methods: Broadcasting seeds, transplanting seedlings (21 days old).

Care

  • Maintain 5–10 cm water during growth.
  • Fertilize with NPK at different stages (tillering, panicle).
  • Control pests (stem borer, leaf folder).

Breeding

Through seeds (can keep seeds from previous harvest).

Harvest

  • 3–4 months depending on variety.
  • Harvest when grains are hard and golden.
