Pumpkin

Planting

  • Soil: Sandy loam with good organic matter.
  • Plant from seeds directly in the field.
  • Spacing: 1–1.5 m between plants.

Care

  • Needs full sun.
  • Apply compost + NPK fertilizer.
  • Requires pollination (bees are important).

Breeding

Propagated by seeds. Can cross-pollinate leading to variation.

Harvest

  • 90–120 days.
  • Harvest when skin is hard and fruit sounds hollow.
