Tea

Planting

  • Climate: Cool, wet (highlands in Sri Lanka ideal).
  • Soil: Acidic, well-drained (pH 4.5–5.5).
  • Propagation: Seedlings or cuttings planted in rows.

Care

  • Regular pruning for bushy growth.
  • Shade trees can help.
  • Fertilize with nitrogen, potassium, and phosphates.

Breeding

Mostly vegetative (cuttings) for uniformity.

Harvest

  • Plucking starts after 3 years.
  • Harvest 2 leaves and a bud (“two leaves and a bud”).
