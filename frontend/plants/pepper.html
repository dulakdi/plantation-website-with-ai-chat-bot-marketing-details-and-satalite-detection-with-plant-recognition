Pepper (Black Pepper / Capsicum)

Planting

  • Black pepper: Climber plant – needs support like a pole/tree.
  • Soil: Well-drained loam soil, pH 5.5–6.5.
  • Propagation: Stem cuttings with 2–3 nodes.

Care

  • Provide shade in early growth.
  • Needs regular irrigation but avoid waterlogging.
  • Apply compost + potassium fertilizer.

Breeding

Vegetative cuttings are best (faster than seeds).

Harvest

  • Starts yielding in 3 years, good yield in 7–8 years.
  • Harvest green pepper spikes before berries turn red.
