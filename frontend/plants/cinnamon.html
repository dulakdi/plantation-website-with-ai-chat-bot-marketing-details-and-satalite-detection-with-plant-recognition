Cinnamon

Planting

  • Soil: Sandy loam with good drainage.
  • Plant during June–July (monsoon).
  • Spacing: about 3 m between trees.

Care

  • Water moderately, avoid waterlogging.
  • Apply organic manure twice yearly.
  • Prune to maintain height and bush shape.
  • Mulch around base to retain moisture.

Breeding

Mostly grown from cuttings or seedlings.

Harvest

  • Harvest bark from 2–3 year old trees.
  • Peel bark in strips during dry season and sun dry.
