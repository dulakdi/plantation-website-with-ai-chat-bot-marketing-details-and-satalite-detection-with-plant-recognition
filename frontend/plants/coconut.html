Coconut

Planting

  • Climate: Tropical, needs full sun.
  • Soil: Sandy loam, well-drained.
  • Plant spacing: 8–10 m apart. Seedlings from healthy nuts (12-month-old).

Care

  • Apply compost yearly.
  • Water deeply in dry seasons.
  • Control weeds around the palm.

Breeding

Propagation through nuts (no vegetative method).

Harvest

  • Starts fruiting at 6–8 years.
  • Produces nuts every month for 60+ years.
