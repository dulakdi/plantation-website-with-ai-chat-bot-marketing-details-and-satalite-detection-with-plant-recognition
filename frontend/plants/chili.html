Chili

Planting

  • Soil: Well-drained loam, pH 6–7.
  • Raised nursery → transplant seedlings at 4–5 weeks.
  • Spacing: 45 × 45 cm.

Care

  • Needs full sunlight.
  • Moderate watering.
  • Fertilize with nitrogen + potassium.
  • Pests: Aphids, thrips, fruit borers (use neem spray).

Breeding

Seeds from mature red chilies. Hybrids for high yield.

Harvest

  • First harvest after 70–90 days.
  • Green chili or dry red chili depending on use.
